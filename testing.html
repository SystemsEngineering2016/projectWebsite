Testing


We know it is vital to test our project in order to make sure it does what it is intended to do. We firstly need to make sure that the client (end user) is happy with the website we have made and its functionality. We then need to make sure that the website matches the requirements as these are the 'contract' we created with our client. In order to test our product in term 2, we will complete a variety of tasks:

User Testing

We will get the client to test the product and give his feedback on what he thinks. We will do this both with a survey and also ask to give generalised verbal feedback

Requirements Testing

We will test the product and make sure it meets the requirements we created. We will go through the following table to make sure our requirements are met:

Functional Requirements
